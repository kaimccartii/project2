Introduction

Tigers are the largest cat species in the world reaching up to 3.3 meters in length and weighing up to 670 pounds. The scientific name of the tiger is Panthera tigris.

Habitat and Behavior

Tigers are found in a variety of habitats in Asia, including forests, grasslands, and wetlands. They are solitary and territorial animals.

Interesting Facts

  • Tigers are excellent swimmers.
  • They can run up to 60 km/h.
  • A tiger's roar can be heard as far as 3 kilometers away.
