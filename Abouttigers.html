About Tigers

Subspecies of Tigers

There are several subspecies of tigers, each adapted to their unique habitats:

  • Bengal Tiger
  • Siberian Tiger
  • Sumatran Tiger
  • Indochinese Tiger
  • Malayan Tiger
  • South China Tiger

Conservation Efforts

Conservation organizations are working tirelessly to protect and preserve tiger populations in the wild. Efforts include anti-poaching measures, habitat preservation, and breeding programs.
